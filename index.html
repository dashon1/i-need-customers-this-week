Quick Answers

Do I need a social media following for this to work?

No. The most effective tactics in this plan — network audit, past client reactivation, and referral blitz — work with zero social following. You only need a phone and a contact list.

I'm a freelancer, not a business owner. Does this apply to me?

Yes — this was built with freelancers and solopreneurs in mind. All the scripts are written for individual service providers and can be adapted for any type of work.

What if I have very few past clients or a small network?

The plan still works with a small network — it just means your per-contact effort needs to be higher. If you have 10 past clients, you reach out to all 10. If you have 5, you reach out to all 5 and rely more heavily on Days 4–6 for new visibility. Days 4 and 5 (local channels and social proof) are specifically designed for people who don't have a large existing audience.

How much time does this take each day?

Each day's plan takes 45–90 minutes if you follow it. Some days (like Day 1 and Day 7) are more about personal outreach and can be done in under an hour. Day 4 (local visibility) requires setting up a Google Business profile if you don't have one, which may take 30–60 extra minutes the first time.

What format does the guide come in?

PDF (all tiers) plus editable Word/Google Docs templates (Complete tier only). Instant download after purchase.
